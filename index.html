/ Who We Are

Built for students,
by students

CampusXchange was born in a hostel room in Lahore, out of frustration with overpriced textbooks and zero learning resources. Today, we're Pakistan's fastest-growing campus ecosystem.

Our Mission

To empower every campus student with affordable resources and in-demand skills — removing financial barriers and creating a thriving peer economy across Pakistan.

Our Vision

To become the definitive campus super-app for South Asia — where every student can buy, sell, learn, and connect in one seamless, trusted environment.

Our Journey

2022 — The Idea

Born in a Lahore hostel room

Frustrated with expensive textbooks, Ali and Sara built the first prototype in a weekend.

2023 — Launch

Public beta at 5 universities

Within 3 months, over 2,000 students joined the platform with 800+ active listings.

2024 — Skill Hub

Launched the learning platform

Added 240+ courses and onboarded 60+ instructors. Pro plan launched.

2025 — Scale

12K+ students across Pakistan

Expanding to 40+ universities. Seed funding secured. The journey continues.
